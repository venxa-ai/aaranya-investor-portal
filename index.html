🥑 Lifestyle Tips
  • Avoid aggressive detangling when waking up; use a seamless neem wood wide-tooth comb.
  • Apply a light hydration mist or botanical water base to stimulate cuticular swelling safely.
  • Protect hair fibers from UV oxidation by wearing a lightweight hat or protective silk scarf if spending extended hours outdoors.
  • Apply 2-3 drops of AARANYA™ Hair Oil exclusively onto dry, frayed ends to lock in overnight hydration and prevent splitting.
  • Perform a gentle 3-minute fingertip cranial manipulation without oil to relax the aponeurosis muscle.
  • Replace cotton pillowcases with premium silk or satin cases to eliminate high frictional dragging coefficients during sleep.
  • Warm up 1-1.5 tablespoons of AARANYA™ Premium Botanical Hair Oil. Warmth expands follicular absorption channels.
  • Section your hair and apply the warm oil systematically directly onto your scalp, massaging gently in circular motions.
  • Leave the oil on for a minimum of 45 minutes, or overnight in a loose wrap, before cleansing. Recommended frequency: 2-3 times per week.
  • Restrict shampoo washings to 2 or 3 times a week max. Over-cleansing strips protective natural sebum.
  • Ensure your shampoo is completely sulfate-free, paraben-free, and pH-balanced to avoid follicle corrosion.
  • Always wash with lukewarm water and finish with a cold water rinse to snap the cuticular shingles shut, locking in shine.
  • Use the 4-minute inversion massage method: bend slightly forward to increase blood flow to follicles.
  • Employ firm, deep circular touchpoints using the pads of your fingers (never nails) starting from occipital lines moving up to the crown.
  • Combine massage with a wooden comb routine to evenly distribute natural scalp oils down the hair shaft.
  • Prioritize dietary protein intake, specifically rich in sulfur-containing amino acids (lentils, pumpkin seeds, walnuts).

AARANYA™ Hair Care Guide

Your comprehensive handbook containing optimized routines, expert strategies, and dietary guidelines to support your complete hair transformation journey.

☀️

Morning Hair Routine

Gently detangle your dry strands with a premium neem wood wide-tooth comb. Spray a lightweight, soothing botanical mist or hydration base directly onto the scalp to refresh follicles and provide an active shield for the day.

🌙

Night Hair Routine

Apply 2-3 drops of AARANYA™ Hair Oil to your ends. Perform a slow, methodical 3-minute scalp massage using fingertip pads to soothe systemic tension and trigger root blood flow. Protect hair with a premium silk or satin pillowcase.

💆

Weekly Oil Massage

Warm 1-1.5 tablespoons of AARANYA™ Premium Botanical Hair Oil. Section your hair meticulously and apply directly onto the scalp. Massage systematically in slow, circular motions. Leave on for at least 45 minutes or overnight before washing.

🧼

Correct Hair Washing Method

Limit washes to 2-3 times per week. Use a mild, sulfate-free, paraben-free, pH-balanced shampoo. Focus shampoo application only on the scalp and let the lather wash down lengths. Always rinse with lukewarm or cold water to lock cuticles.

🥗

Healthy Diet Tips

Prioritize clean, sulfur-rich proteins (lentils, walnuts, pumpkin seeds, almonds). Ensure stable intake of spinach, iron-rich leafy greens, and citrus fruits packed with Vitamin C to assist direct collagen production and iron absorption.

🧘‍♂️

Lifestyle & Stress Care

Drink 2.5 to 3 liters of water daily. Integrate proven stress management tools like daily deep breathing, mindfulness, or yogic breathing (Anulom Vilom) since elevated cortisol blocks hair-follicle growth cycles.

⚠️ Common Hair Care Mistakes to Avoid

  • • Washing with hot water (strips natural scalp oils and leaves hair dry and brittle).
  • • Sleeping with damp or wet hair (causes hair shaft swelling and leads to micro-breakage).
  • • Aggressive towel drying (roughens cuticles, leading to split ends and immediate hair breakage).
